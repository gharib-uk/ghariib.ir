Entries Now Open for the Asia Pacific Software Testing Awards 2025

07.01.2025 00:00 devsecops digital-transformation expert-pov ndc

Bangalore, India – The Asia Pacific Software Testing Awards 2025 is now accepting entries, offering an unparalleled opportunity to celebrate excellence in digital technologies. This prestigious awards program recognises the outstanding achievements of businesses, teams, and individuals across the Asia Pacific region and the UAE who have demonstrated exceptional innovation and commitment to enhancing business processes, customer experience, or cultural transformation.

The 15 diverse categories include:

  1. Best Agile Project

  2. Diversity and Inclusion Award

  3. Best Advancing Software Testing Practice

  4. Best Test Automation Project – Functional & Non-Functional

  5. Best Use of Technology in a Project

  6. Testing Newcomer (Champion / Team / Leader of the Year)

  7. Best Overall Testing Project (Electronic Communication / Finance / Public Sector & Services / Retail)

  8. Most Innovative Project

  9. Leading Vendor – Tools & Technology / Service Delivery & Consulting

The awards showcase accomplishments across various domains. The deadline for entries is 28th May 2025, with finalists enjoying the chance to celebrate their success at an exclusive awards ceremony.

Click here to enter The 2025 Asia Pacific Software Testing Awards

Judged with complete impartiality and transparency, each submission is anonymised to ensure fairness, allowing projects to be evaluated solely on their merit.

Key benefits of participation include:

  • Gaining industry recognition for projects, teams, or individuals.

  • Expanding your professional network at the gala dinner and winner’s ceremony.

  • Building market credibility as an award-winning organisation.
